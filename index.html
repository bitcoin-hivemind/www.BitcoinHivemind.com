Bitcoin "Smart Contracts" (As Smart As We Are)

Open Source Your Knowledge

Hivemind is a Peer-to-Peer Oracle Protocol which absorbs accurate data into a blockchain so that Bitcoin-users can speculate in Prediction Markets. These markets have many applications (which include superior alternatives to Lighthouse, Counterparty, Medici, Bitshares, and Ethereum) and have the potential to revolutionize the emergence and diffusion of knowledge in society.

If you've never even heard of Bitcoin before, try What is Hivemind? (10 fun slides) to learn about what you've been missing this whole time. Those already familiar with Bitcoin 2.0 projects might enjoy What makes Hivemind different?

The Goal: Scientific Revolution Part II

Speaking technically, this project aims to get external data into a blockchain reliably, and use that data efficiently and conservatively.

However, unlike other blockchain-projects, the ultimate purpose is NOT to establish a new payment system (nor to delude the innocent with get-rich-quick nonsense), but instead to...

  • ...revolutionize the way information is created and spread, giving the internet a "frontal lobe"...
  • ...overhaul democratic governance (deemphasizing showmanship/PR, highlighting the irrelevance of race/gender/who-candidate-has-slept-with)...
  • ...boost economic growth...
  • ...provide a novel way to efficiently fund public goods, without taxation or coercion...
  • ...cut out all Wall St. / banking middlemen and replace them with tech...
  • ...spearhead the campaign to make Bitcoin the world's internet currency...
  • ...and more!
